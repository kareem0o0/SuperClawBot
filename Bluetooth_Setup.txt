Bluetooth Pairing Guide (Linux)

1. Enable Bluetooth
    sudo systemctl start bluetooth
    bluetoothctl

3. Pair and Connect
    pair 00:23:00:01:60:9C
    trust 00:23:00:01:60:9C
    connect 00:23:00:01:60:9C

4. Create Serial Port
    Exit bluetoothctl, then bind the serial port:
    sudo rfcomm bind 0 00:23:00:01:60:9C

Now your device is available at /dev/rfcomm0.
